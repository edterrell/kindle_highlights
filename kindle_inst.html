Kindle Highlights Viewer

A simple tool to explore your Kindle highlights either through a web app or on your own machine.

Use the App Online (No Setup Needed)

You can try the app directly in your browser, no installation required.

Launch the app:
https://kindle.streamlit.app

How it works:

  • Export your Kindle highlights file (usually named My Clippings.txt) from your device.
  • Upload that file into the app.
  • Browse random quotes, search by title, or view all your books.

Features:

  • View a random highlight with its local context
  • View all highlights from a selected book
  • See a full list of books (title, author, and year read)

Using Your Highlights:

Connect your Kindle to your computer via USB. Copy the My Clippings.txt file from the Kindle documents folder.

For convenience, save it to your desktop or somewhere easy to find. When prompted by the app, upload the file.
